Bio

Hi there! I'm a CS Ph.D student at Rice University. My advisor is Dr. Xia Ben Hu. I got my B.S. degree in physics from University of Science and Technology of China(USTC). I previously worked with Dr. Ang Chen on network system and security.

Research Interests

I design efficient machine learning algorithms and systems using techniques such as sparsification and speculative decoding, while improving system robustness and security. My research centers on LLM post-training, including efficiency, long-context, LLM agents, routing, and security.
  • Efficiency and long-context LLMs. [HedgeSpec] [ProxSparse] [KVBench] [AutoL2S] [StopOverthinking]
  • RAG, LLM safety, LLM Agent, LLM Routing. [LoRATK] [RouterArena] [Router] [Chain-of-query]
  • Before LLM era: efficient machine learning and applications. [StructDrop] [RMT]
  • Systems and network. [RDMI] [Bedrock] [Runtime] [Cloudless]
