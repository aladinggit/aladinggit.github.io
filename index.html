Bio

Hi there! I'm a CS Ph.D student at Rice University. My advisor is Dr. Xia Ben Hu. I got my B.S. degree in physics from University of Science and Technology of China(USTC). My current research interests are in machine learning system and security, with a particular focus on LLM efficiency and security. I previously worked with Dr. Ang Chen on network system and security.
